## PART II — COOKIE POLICY

### 8. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files that a website saves in a visitor's browser. They allow sites to remember information between visits.

### 9. Cookies Used by This Site

okhodjaev.com uses the following categories of cookies.

**9.1. Strictly Necessary**

Cookies that enable basic Site functionality. The reading mode preference (light, dark, or sepia theme) is saved in the browser's localStorage. This data is not transmitted anywhere and is stored only in your browser. These cannot be disabled without loss of functionality.

**9.2. Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)**

Google Analytics sets the following cookies:

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Distinguishes unique users | 2 years |
| `_ga_*` | Stores session state | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Distinguishes users | 24 hours |
| `_gat` | Limits request frequency | 1 minute |

These cookies are set on the basis of legitimate interest or, where required by applicable law, with your consent.

### 10. How to Opt Out of Cookies

You may manage cookies in the following ways:

- **Browser settings** — most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Instructions: [aboutcookies.org](https://www.aboutcookies.org)
- **Google Analytics Opt-out** — install the official Google browser add-on: [tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- **Google Privacy Controls** — [myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy](https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy)

Opting out of analytics cookies does not affect access to the content of the Site.
